Chapter One
Our Story
Arizona · 1994 → Present

"Traditional home selling was broken. Sellers were losing months, thousands in fees, and their peace of mind — all at once. We built Wrangler Homes to fix that."

— James Wrangler, Founder · Scottsdale, Arizona
The Problem

For decades, selling a home meant months of open houses, agent commissions eating 5–6% of your sale price, buyers whose financing falls through at the last minute, and repair demands that eat into your net. Families in distress had no real alternative.

Our Answer

In 1994, James and Carol Wrangler closed their first two cash purchases in Scottsdale. No agent, no listing, no waiting. One call, one written offer, one closing. The response was immediate — word spread fast that there was finally a better way.

Today

Over 12,000 homes later, the mission is unchanged. We buy directly, for cash, in all 50 states — with the same integrity as that first deal in Arizona. Every number we publish represents a real family we served with honesty.
