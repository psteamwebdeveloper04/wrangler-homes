Summary

Wrangler Homes respects your privacy. We collect only what we need to evaluate your property and make you a cash offer. We do not sell your personal information to third parties — ever.

01 Introduction

Who We Are

Wrangler Homes LLC ("Wrangler Homes," "we," "us," or "our") is a real estate investment company headquartered at 7890 Desert Blvd, Scottsdale, AZ 85251. We purchase residential and commercial properties directly from homeowners across all fifty states of the United States.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect the personal information you share with us when you visit our website, submit a property inquiry, call our team, or interact with us in any other way. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

If you have any questions before reading further, contact us at sales@wranglerhomes.com or call 1 (877) 269-2267.

02 Data Collection

Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways: directly from you, and automatically through your use of our website.

Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact details — your first name, last name, phone number, and email address.
  • Property information — the address, type, condition, and estimated value of the property you wish to sell.
  • Situation details — any voluntary context you provide (foreclosure, inherited property, relocation, divorce, etc.).
  • Selling timeline — how quickly you need or wish to complete a sale.
  • Communications — records of emails, call transcripts (where permitted by law), and any messages you send us.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device & browser data — browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and device type.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, links clicked, and referring URLs.
  • IP address — used to determine approximate geographic location (city/state level only).
  • Cookies & local storage — see Section 5 for full details.
Note

We do not collect Social Security numbers, financial account details, credit scores, or government ID numbers at any point during our online process.

03 Purpose of Collection

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

PurposeDetails
Evaluate your propertyTo assess market value and prepare a fair cash offer based on your property details.
Contact youTo follow up by phone, email, or SMS with your cash offer or any questions about your property.
Process transactionsTo facilitate the purchase agreement, closing, and related legal paperwork once an offer is accepted.
Improve our servicesTo analyze how visitors use our website so we can improve content, layout, and user experience.
Legal complianceTo comply with applicable real estate laws, tax requirements, and regulatory obligations.
SecurityTo detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and other malicious activity.

We will never use your information for purposes that conflict with the reason you provided it. We do not send unsolicited commercial emails to people who have not expressed interest in our services.

04 Third Parties

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Period. We may share your information only in the limited circumstances described below:

  • Service providers — trusted vendors who help us operate our website under strict data processing agreements.
  • Title companies & closing agents — if you accept an offer, we share necessary information with the title company and escrow agent handling the closing.
  • Legal & regulatory — if required by a valid court order, subpoena, or applicable law.
  • Business transfers — if Wrangler Homes is acquired or merges, your information may transfer as part of that transaction.
  • With your consent — in any other case where you have given us explicit permission.
Our Commitment

We will never share your information with real estate agents, marketing companies, data brokers, or lead resellers. Your inquiry stays private between you and Wrangler Homes.

06 Retention

Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

  • Inquiry data — retained for up to 3 years after your last contact, or until you request deletion.
  • Closed transaction records — retained for 7 years to meet legal and accounting requirements.
  • Website analytics data — retained for up to 26 months in anonymized form.
  • Email communications — retained for up to 3 years for quality assurance.

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it in accordance with our internal retention schedule.

07 Your Rights

Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have some or all of the following rights:

  • Right to Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Deletion — request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out — opt out of any marketing communications at any time.
  • Right to Portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — exercising these rights will not affect the quality of service you receive.
California Residents — CCPA

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, to delete it, to opt out of its sale (we don't sell it), and to non-discrimination. Submit requests to sales@wranglerhomes.com.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sales@wranglerhomes.com. We will respond within 30 days.

08 Security

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. Our security practices include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted through our website.
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive information at rest.
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular security assessments and staff training on data protection.
  • Secure deletion protocols when data is no longer needed.

No system is completely secure. If you believe your information has been compromised, please contact us immediately at privacy@wranglerhomes.com.

09 Minors

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended solely for adults who are of legal age to enter into a real estate transaction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately at sales@wranglerhomes.com. We will promptly delete any such information upon verification.

10 External Sites

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites such as title company portals, state government property databases, or social media platforms. These sites are not operated by Wrangler Homes, and we have no control over their privacy practices.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit. Wrangler Homes is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external sites.

11 Updates

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or business operations. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Post a notice on our homepage for 30 days following a significant change.
  • Send an email notification to users who have an active relationship with us.

Your continued use of our services after any changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
