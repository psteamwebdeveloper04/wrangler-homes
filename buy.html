Got Questions?

The Answers You Actually Need

Is your cash offer really free with no obligation?
Completely. We don't charge anything at any stage. Getting an offer from Wrangler Homes costs you nothing — not even your time. The whole initial assessment takes under 10 minutes, and you're never pressured to accept. If you don't like the offer, you walk away with no strings attached.
How do you determine the offer price?
We analyze recent comparable sales in your area, the current condition of your property, local market trends, and your preferred timeline. Our offer reflects a fair market value adjusted for the speed and certainty we provide. You avoid agent fees (5–6%), repair costs, and carrying costs — many sellers net more with us than a traditional sale.
What if my home needs major repairs or has serious problems?
That's exactly who we're designed for. We buy homes with foundation problems, roof damage, fire damage, water damage, code violations, mold, and every other issue you can think of. We price the offer fairly accounting for the condition — you never have to fix or clean anything. Leave it exactly as it is.
How fast can you really close?
Our record is 4 days from first call to cash in hand. Realistically, most sellers close in 7–14 days when they want speed. There's no financing to wait on — we use our own cash. If you need more time, we can close up to 90 days out. You set the date; we build around it.
What happens after I accept the offer?
We handle everything from that point forward. A title company is engaged, all paperwork is prepared, and you're guided through the signing process — in person or fully remote if you prefer. On the closing date, you sign the final documents and receive your funds. That's it. We even handle cleanout of any belongings left behind.
Do you buy in my state?
Yes — all 50 states, including Hawaii and Alaska. We've been expanding steadily since 1994 and reached full national coverage in 2012. Whether you're in rural Montana or downtown Miami, we have agents and cash reserves available to move on your property.
